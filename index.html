Info

The Wide Use of Cellphones:

It is estimated that there are 7 billion cellphone users out of 7.5 billion around the world.

Apps:

There are over 2.1 million apps to choose from, and it has encouraged people to perform multiple activities simultaneously. Due to these features, there is an increasing tendency for people to multitask such as eating and texting.

The Rule of Three

“In a conversation among five or six people at dinner, you have to check that three people are paying attention — heads up — before you give yourself permission to look down at your phone” (Turkle, 2015). This shows that different people would have their heads up at different times.

Effects:

reduction in face-to-face conversations limits one’s ability to hold deep conversations

Bottomline

In order to avoid further social declination, cell phones should be used with greater intention.
